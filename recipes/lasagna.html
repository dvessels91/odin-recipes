Lasagna

Description of lasagna

Lasagna is pasta

Lasagna takes immense skill to create

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Ground Beef
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Boil the pasta until al dente
  2. Cook ground beef and tomato sauce for 30 minutes
  3. Layer pasta and beef sauce
  4. Bake at 375 for 1 hour
